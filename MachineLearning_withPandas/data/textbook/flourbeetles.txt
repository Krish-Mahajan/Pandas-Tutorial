These data are used in the book "Computational Statistics" 
by G.H. Givens and J.A. Hoeting.

Name: flourbeetles.dat
Chapter: 2
Discussed in: Problem 2.6

Source: Royal N. Chapman, "The Quantitative Analysis of Environmental
Factors," Ecology, Vol 9, No 2, (April 1928), 111-122.

Description: Counts of a flour beetle (Tribolium confusum) population
at various points in time (days).  Beetles in all stages of
development were counted, and food supply was carefully controlled.

Variable labels: days, beetles
