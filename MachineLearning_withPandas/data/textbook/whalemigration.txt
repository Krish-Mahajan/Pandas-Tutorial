These data are used in the book "Computational Statistics" 
by G.H. Givens and J.A. Hoeting.

Name: whalemigration.dat
Chapter: 10
Discussed in: Examples 10.2, 10.3, 10.4, 10.6

Source: Geof Givens, Colorado State University

Description: Sighting times for 121 bowhead whale calves passing Point
Barrow, Alaska, during the 2001 spring migration.  Times are expressed
as hours since midnight, April 5, when the first adult whale was
spotted during the visual census effort.
