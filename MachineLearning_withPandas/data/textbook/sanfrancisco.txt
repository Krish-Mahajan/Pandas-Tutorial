These data are used in the book "Computational Statistics" 
by G.H. Givens and J.A. Hoeting.

Name: sanfrancisco.dat
Chapter: 1
Discussed in: Example 1.3

Source:  Jan Null, Golden Gate Weather Services, Climate of San Francisco, 
	 [Online] ggweather.com/sf/climate.html

Description: Rainfall amounts (inches) for San Francisco for Novermber
through March from November 1990 until March 2002.  During these
months, San Francisco typically receives more than 80% of its
precipitation.  A zero entry indicates that less than 0.01 inches of
precipitation fell on that day.  

Variable labels: Month, Day, Year, Amount
